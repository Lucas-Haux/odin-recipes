Strawberry Bellini

The Strawberry Bellini is a refreshing and effervescent summer cocktail that perfectly captures the sweetness of ripe strawberries. Its luscious flavors and elegant presentation make it the ideal indulgence for any sophisticated taste.

Ingredients

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Prosecco or sparkling wine
  • Sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Clean and hull the fresh strawberries, then puree them in a blender or food processor until smooth.
  2. Add a tablespoon of sugar and a splash of lemon juice to the strawberry puree, and mix well.
  3. Fill a champagne flute or wine glass halfway with the strawberry mixture.
  4. Top the glass with chilled Prosecco or sparkling wine, leaving enough room for a few ice cubes.
  5. Add a few ice cubes to the glass, and gently stir to combine all the flavors.
  6. Garnish the drink with a fresh strawberry or a slice of lemon, if desired.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
